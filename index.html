I'm Kiran

Hi! I'm 17 years old, studying biotechnology engineering at UC San Diego and here's what I'm pissed about + what I can't get enough of.

Health

I'm pissed. I passionately disagree with the existing healthcare system and wholeheartedly confide in biotech. Biotech is far more holistic and in cases, non-invasive. My mission, with biotech, is to make great healthy possible again.

Gender Equality

I'm pissed. Equality is active, and demanding! To bridge mental and even biological inequalities, we must innovate. I'm working on one such solution with Matrea: developing artificial wombs for external pregnancies.

Yogic Sciences

I'm sold. Every morning, I spend two hours in heaven. A 90 minute yoga session followed by a 30 minute meditation gives me the best start to my day. I have a dream to one day do yoga in the Himalayas. Nothing beats nature!

Philosophy

"He who cannot draw on three thousand years is living from hand to mouth." This quote by Poet Goethe is so beautiful. Philosophy is a medium used to study intangibles, intangibles which are vital in this uncertain world.

Experience

  • CEO of CrossBow Miles Canada Inc.

    I'm the Founder and CEO of CrossBow Miles Canada: a movement inspiring Canadian youth to launch passion projects which help educate women and girl's in India. I head a team of 30 high school students. Together, we run monetarily and mission driven projects to fund building toilets in Indian schools. The lack of toilets in Indian schools is one of the primary reasons a young girls won't attend.

  • Founder of Matrea

    Warning: it's a moonshot. At Matrea, my co-founder and I are working to develop artificial wombs for external pregnancy. Childbirth is a miracle but it's imperfect, dangerous to both mother and child. At Matrea, we #MimicTheMiracle. Driven by our mission to bridge the biological inequality between the sexes, we're starting by developing breast milk alternatives, and giving 'extra womb time' to premature babies. Eventually, a Matrea womb will be a safer alternative to traditional pregnancy.

Education

  • UC San Diego

    Biotechnology Engineering is unique to this institution; it not only provides a solid foundation as an engineering, it also has a lot more biology and chemistry than a typical biomedical engineering program. Only bioengineering majors are permitted to take these department courses; labs to apply your knowledge are an integral aspect to the program. In addition, a minimum of 10 humanities courses must be taken to ensure a well rounded student. This degree is optimal for cutting-edge biotechnology startups looking for combination of a scientific and technical degree.

  • Personal Pursuits

    Learning should not be limited to the classroom. My curiousity has me reading research papers on topics ranging from anti-aging, quantum biology, to the hypothalamus! I've produced a number of articles and videos on these topics on Medium and YouTube.
